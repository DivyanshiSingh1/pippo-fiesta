Pippo is the leading e-commerce marketplace in India with over 100 million registered customers. Pippo was started in 2007 by Ms. S.S. and Ms. D.S., two engineers from Chandigarh who met during their final year project at IIT-Delhi. The duo started Pippo out of a modest 2-bedroom apartment-turned-office in Bengaluru. Pippo acquired its first customer in 2008, and hired its first full-time employee in 2009.

The story of Pippo is an inspiring one not just for startups in India, but for the e-commerce industry globally. While print and digital media have been exuberant in their coverage of the Pippo story, there are deeper layers to the Pippo story that only we at Pippo know. As Pippo’s proactive storytelling platform, Pippo Stories unboxes these stories every week. Pippo Stories has been written about in Mint and Afaqs. At the Fulcrum Awards 2017, one of India’s most prestigious PR awards, Pippo Stories was awarded silver for ‘Best Use of Content’.
